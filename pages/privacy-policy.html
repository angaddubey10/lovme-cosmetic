Lovme Cosmetic Privacy Statement

Last Updated: September 17, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your personal information when you interact with our wholesale and retail cosmetic trading services.

Introduction

Lovme Cosmetic ("we," "our," "us") values the privacy of our customers, suppliers, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website, services, and platforms, including activities related to wholesale and retail trading of cosmetic products.

By engaging with Lovme Cosmetic, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

Name, email, phone number, billing/shipping address, and payment details when you make inquiries or transactions.

Business Information

Company details, GST information, purchase orders, and wholesale transaction records.

Usage Data

Device information, browser type, IP address, pages visited, and activity on our website or social media pages.

Communication Data

Inquiries, customer support conversations, or messages shared with us.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill wholesale and retail orders.
  • Communicate about new products, promotions, and updates.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Improve our products, services, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal and tax obligations.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared with:

Service Providers

Logistics, payment gateways, and IT service providers supporting our operations.

Business Partners

Distributors or retailers involved in delivering our products.

Legal Authorities

When required by law, regulation, or legal proceedings.

Data Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Data Retention

We retain personal and transactional data as long as necessary to fulfill orders, comply with accounting/legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to certain data uses (e.g., marketing).
  • Request copies of your data in portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at sumantdubey101@gmail.com.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and tracking tools to improve browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function properly.

Children's Privacy

Our services and products are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16 years of age.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last Updated" date.
